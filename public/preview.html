General information +

Arizona Traffic Survival School is a one-day, eight-hour class for people who voluntarily attend or are required to attend by the MVD or a court. Classes run 8:00 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. and are offered in English and Spanish, both in person and virtually via Zoom.

This interactive course includes presentations, videos, and workbook exercises. Active participation is mandatory for course credit. We electronically notify the Arizona MVD upon completion, and your instructor provides a completion certificate for your records.

In-person class details +
  • Arrival time: 7:45 a.m. for check-in
  • Cost: $139 (exact amount required)

Required at check-in:

  • Valid photo ID
  • Payment or proof of payment

Payment options:

  • Invoice (must be requested in advance)
  • Money order (payable to $139 AZ Discount TSS)
  • Cash (exact amount only — no change provided)
Virtual class requirements +

Prerequisites:

  • A one-time waiver from the Arizona Chapter, National Safety Council (602-222-3381)
  • Contact us after receiving waiver approval to register

Cost structure:

  • Base cost: $139
  • Shipping & handling: $10 (for workbook delivery)
  • Typical total: $149

Required materials: A physical workbook (no digital version available).

Workbook pickup options:

  • In-person pickup ($139 payment required)
  • FedEx delivery to a physical address (no PO boxes)
Technology requirements (virtual) +
  • Check-in deadline: 7:30 a.m. (Phoenix, Arizona MST)
  • Equipment: Computer or tablet (preferred over phone), reliable internet, webcam and microphone
  • Power: A power source for the full 8-hour duration
  • Software: The latest version of Zoom
Virtual class conduct guidelines +

Environment:

  • A quiet, distraction-free space with a neutral background
  • Professional attire (no offensive content)
  • Must be at a desk or table (no participating from bed)
  • No simultaneous activities (work, childcare, etc.)

Participation:

  • Must be visible and audible throughout class
  • Full attention required; class may be monitored or recorded

Important: You must arrange childcare in advance, as full attention and participation are required throughout the class.

The instructor reserves the right to enforce the dress code and participation requirements. Technical preparation, including Zoom familiarity, is the participant's responsibility. Zoom video tutorials are available for reference.
